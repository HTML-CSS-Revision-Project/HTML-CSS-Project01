Somalia Coastline

Somalia has the longest coastline in mainland Africa, stretching over 3,300 kilometers. Its coastal waters are rich in marine biodiversity, supporting fishing communities and beautiful natural landscapes. From the white sands of Berbera to the coral reefs of Kismayo, Somalia's coastline holds historical trade routes, untouched beauty, and great economic potential.
